About Me

Hello! 👋 I’m glad you’re here. This site is where I share what I have been up to.

I am a treasury quant analyst for Centiva Capital, a multistrategy hedge fund located in New York City. I graduated from USC with degrees in Computer Science, Applied Math, and Business Administration. I spend most of my free time reading and investing to achieve superior risk-adjusted returns in order to fund capital-intensive hobbies such as traveling, poker, and golf.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about health and fitness — I love running, winter sports, and weightlifting. This website is a space for me to share projects, thoughts, and progress across the areas that matter to me most.
